Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

IP Transit LLC ("IP Transit," "we," "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Mobile information. We do not sell or rent your personal information. Personal information, including mobile phone numbers and SMS consent, is never shared with third parties or affiliates for their marketing or promotional purposes. Information collected as part of an SMS/text messaging consent process is not shared with any third parties for any purpose.

Information we collect

We collect information you provide directly, such as your name, email address, mobile phone number, and the contents of messages you send us; and basic technical information (such as IP address and device/browser type) when you use our website or services.

How we use information

We use information to provide and operate our services, to send service-related and account messages (including SMS where you have consented), to respond to inquiries, to maintain security, and to comply with legal obligations.

SMS / text messaging

If you consent to receive text messages from IP Transit, we use your mobile number only to send the messages you signed up for. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out at any time, or HELP for help. See our SMS Terms for details. Consent to receive SMS is not a condition of any purchase.

Sharing

We share information only with service providers who process it on our behalf under confidentiality obligations, or when required by law. We do not sell personal information, and we do not share mobile opt-in data or phone numbers with third parties for marketing.

Data retention & security

We retain information for as long as needed to provide the service and meet legal requirements, and we use reasonable safeguards to protect it.

Your choices

You may opt out of SMS by replying STOP. You may request access to or deletion of your information by contacting us.
